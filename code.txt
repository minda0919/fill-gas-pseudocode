#A function to fill your car with gas assuming you're already parked at the station

Fill car with gas
	Exit car
	Open car's gas tank
	Insert credit or debit card into card reader
	Select price limit on card reader
	Take out gas nozzle from machine
	Insert gas nozzle into car's gas tank
	Select gas type
	WHILE gas tank is NOT full OR price limit is NOT reached
		Engage gas nozzle
